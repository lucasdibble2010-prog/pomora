Put your ambient sound files here.

Required filenames (the app loads these by name):
  lofi.mp3
  rain.mp3
  cafe.mp3
  forest.mp3
  fireplace.mp3

Tips:
- Must be .mp3 and named exactly as above.
- Looping tracks work best (the player loops them automatically).
- To add MORE tracks: drop the .mp3 in here, then open index.html and add an
  entry to the SOUNDS list near the top of the <script>, e.g.:
      { name: "Ocean", file: "ocean.mp3" },
- You can delete this README once your sounds are in.
