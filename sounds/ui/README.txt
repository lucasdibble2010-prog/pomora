Put your BUTTON CLICK sound here (plays when you press Start/Pause, Reset,
mode tabs, background next/prev, and the settings tabs).

Required filename (the app loads this by name):
  click.mp3

Tips:
- Must be .mp3 and named exactly "click.mp3" (or change UI_CLICK_FILE in the
  <script> if you use a different name).
- Use a VERY short, soft click (50-150 ms) so rapid presses feel snappy.
- Volume: change UI_CLICK_VOLUME in index.html (0 = silent, 1 = full).
- Mute without deleting: Settings -> Alerts tab -> "UI click sounds" toggle.
- Until you add the file, clicks are silently ignored (nothing breaks).
- You can delete this README once your sound is in.
