Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Welcome to Pomora ("Pomora", "we", "us", or "our"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

You can use Pomora without creating an account.

When using Pomora as a guest

We may store the following information locally in your browser:

  • Timer settings and preferences.
  • Focus session history.
  • Productivity statistics and streaks.
  • Theme and notification preferences.

This information remains on your device unless you choose to delete it.

When you create an account

If you choose to sign in, we may collect and store:

  • Your name or username (if provided).
  • Your email address.
  • Your timer settings and preferences.
  • Focus session history and productivity statistics.

This information is used to synchronize your data across devices and restore your progress when you sign in.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Save your settings and preferences.
  • Display your focus history and productivity reports.
  • Synchronize your data across devices if you have an account.
  • Improve the functionality and reliability of Pomora.
  • Respond to support requests if you contact us.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Storage and Security

Guest user data is stored locally in your browser.

If you create an account, your account information and synced data are stored securely on our servers or through our authentication and database provider.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

4. Sharing With Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We may use trusted third-party services to operate Pomora, such as authentication, cloud storage, analytics, or hosting providers. These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their services.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Use Pomora without creating an account.
  • Delete locally stored data by clearing your browser storage.
  • Update or delete your account information (if account deletion is available).
  • Contact us to request deletion of your account and associated data.

6. Children's Privacy

Pomora is not intended to collect personal information from children under the age required by applicable law. If we learn that such information has been collected without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Pomora after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
