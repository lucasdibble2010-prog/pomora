Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Welcome to Pomora ("Pomora", "we", "us", or "our"), a free Pomodoro focus-timer web application available at pomoras.com. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

You can use the core Pomora timer without creating an account.

When using Pomora as a guest

We may store the following information locally in your browser:

  • Timer settings and preferences.
  • Focus session history.
  • Productivity statistics and streaks.
  • Theme, background, and notification preferences.

This information remains on your device unless you choose to delete it.

When you sign in with Google

If you choose to sign in, we collect and store:

  • Your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • Your timer settings and preferences.
  • Focus session history, tasks, and experience points (XP).

This information is used to synchronize your data across devices and restore your progress when you sign in. It is stored using Google Firebase.

2. Google Calendar and Google Tasks

Pomora offers optional integrations that only activate if you explicitly connect them in Settings:

  • Google Calendar — when enabled, Pomora creates calendar events to log your completed focus sessions.
  • Google Tasks — when enabled, Pomora reads and updates your task list to keep it in sync with the app.

We request only the access required for these features, and only while you have them turned on. You can disconnect them at any time.

Pomora's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not sell your Google user data, we do not use it for advertising, and we do not use it for any purpose other than providing the features you have enabled.

3. Study Rooms and Leaderboard

Some features are social by design:

  • Study Rooms — when you join a room, your display name, current focus time, and status (focusing, on break, or idle) are visible in real time to other people in that same room.
  • Leaderboard — if you are signed in, your display name, level, total focus time, and session count may appear on the public leaderboard.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Save your settings and preferences.
  • Display your focus history and productivity reports.
  • Synchronize your data across devices when you are signed in.
  • Power the Study Room and leaderboard features you choose to use.
  • Log focus sessions to Google Calendar and sync Google Tasks, if connected.
  • Improve the functionality and reliability of Pomora.

We do not sell your personal information.

5. Storage and Security

Guest user data is stored locally in your browser.

If you sign in, your account information and synced data are stored securely through Google Firebase. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Sharing With Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

We rely on trusted third-party services to operate Pomora — primarily Google Firebase (authentication and database) and Google's APIs for the optional integrations. These providers only receive the information necessary to perform their services and handle it under their own privacy policies.

7. Your Choices and Rights

You may:

  • Use Pomora without creating an account.
  • Delete locally stored data by clearing your browser storage.
  • Disconnect Google Calendar/Tasks, or revoke Pomora's access entirely at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Contact us to request deletion of your account and associated data.

8. Children's Privacy

Pomora is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If we learn that such information has been collected without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of Pomora after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
